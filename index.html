The Crystal Collector Game

Just like Jim Henson, Walter White, and most ABC Bachelors, your mind in this game should be on the crystals.

Each game, you will be given a different random target number and your goal is to hit that number by clicking on the crystal skulls below. No one knows the value of the magical crystal skulls ahead of time, so click wisely.

Win the game by hitting the target number, lose the game by going over.
